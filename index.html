ABOUT LA TROBE eBUREAU

The La Trobe eBureau publishes engaging, high quality, open-access etextbooks. The mission of the eBureau is: to elevate La Trobe University to be a major player in open access education; to promote the expertise and scholarship of La Trobe academics; and to reduce resource costs for students by providing open access textbooks, available anytime and anywhere.

All eBureau resources are written by La Trobe University academics for La Trobe courses and subjects. The La Trobe eBureau provides academics with editing, design, copyright and online publishing services. Academics interested in developing open access etextbooks to support the curriculum should contact Fiona Salisbury or Kathy Russell to discuss their publishing needs.
